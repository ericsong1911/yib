About yib

Welcome to yib, a classic-style, self-hosted imageboard project built with Go. It was written in 2025 by Eric Song JE '28.

If you have any suggestions or bug reports you may email the webmaster at e(dot)song(at)yale(dot)edu.

Rules

Follow human decency. That's it. Moderation is done on this basis alone.
